UZH FPV Dataset

We introduce the UZH-FPV Drone Racing dataset, which is the most aggressive visual-inertial odometry dataset to date. Large accelerations, rotations, and apparent motion in vision sensors make aggressive trajectories difficult for state estimation. However, many compelling applications, such as autonomous drone racing, require high speed state estimation, but existing datasets do not address this.
